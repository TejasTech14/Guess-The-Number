Remeninig Chances

  • You Have 10 Chances to Guess The Correct Number

  • The goal is to guess a randomly generated number within a specified range (e.g., 1 to 100).

  • If your guess is too low, you’ll see: "User Entered Input is less than the Random Number"

  • If your guess is too high, you’ll see: "User Entered Input is greater than the Random Number"

  • If your guess is correct, you’ll see: "Yessss! Your Guess is Correct!"

  • You win if you guess the correct number within the allowed attempts.

  • If you use all attempts without guessing correctly, you lose, and the correct number is revealed.
